Association History
Omani Students Association was created in 2013 at olemiss.It consistes of group of omani students at Olemiss.
The main purpose of the Association is to introduce for students our traditions and national big events to give them a good knowledge about Oman

Association Members
Omani students association consists of 25 members.All members are students at the university of Mississippi. Each member has a specific role in the association. The association has many different committee for example,sport,cooking,fianancial, and phptograph

Association Activities
Omani students association has three meetinges every semester. Members of the association usually gather weekly specialy in the weekend.Omanis studesnts association celebrate a big national event which is the National Day of Oman at ford center.
